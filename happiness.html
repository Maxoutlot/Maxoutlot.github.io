How Happy Was I Today?

Welcome to my happiness tracker! Click on any date in the calendar to rate how happy I was that day and add a note about why.

How to use:

  1. Click on any date in the calendar
  2. Use the slider to rate your happiness (1-10)
  3. Add an optional note about why you felt that way
  4. Click "Save Rating" to record it

Color Guide:

  • Red (1-3): Unhappy days
  • Orange (4-6): Neutral days
  • Green (7-10): Happy days
